Our Services

Wound Dressing & Removal

No need to go back to the hospital or clinic to replace or remove wound dressings, as you can consult with our qualified health practioners for a quick and efficient service. We also provide a care plan for how you should be taking care of your dressing, or post removal at home.

Suture & Staples Removal

We provide top-quality suture, and staples post-surgery removal, with a consultation prior to the procedure.
