    About Us

    Our History

    IncMed is a family-owned mobile clinic with practitioners of over 20+ experience in healthcare. What started as a small idea, became an operable vision in helping our community. We provide services specifically for post-surgery and post-wound maintenance, to ensure the healing process is convenient and comfortable for you. We care about our community, and our aim is to be with you along the way. Our staff is professional, experienced and passionate about their work.
