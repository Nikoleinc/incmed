    Welcome to IncMed

    Let us do all of the work

    We offer door-to-door quality medical services for wound dressing and suture removal in the comfort of your home. Your health is our priority.

    DOOR-TO-DOOR SERVICE

    The main purpose of our mobile clinic is to provide door-to-door service for our patients. Our aim is to improve your health and offer top quality service in the comfort of your home.

    CONSULTATION INCLUDED

    A consultation is provided with each service to ensure transparency and communication. The patient will also receive a care plan post removal, to ensure optimum healing.

    Our Services

    Wound Dressing & Removal

    No need to go back to the hospital or clinic to replace or remove wound dressings, as you can consult with our qualified health practioners for a quick and efficient service. Our wound dressing service covers door-to-door, and we will perform the simple non-evasive sterile procedure in the comfort of your home. We also provide a care plan for how you should be taking care of your dressing, or post removal at home.

    Suture & Staples Removal

    We provide top-quality suture, and staples post-surgery removal, with a consultation prior to the procedure. This is a door-to-door serivce, in which our health practitioners will perform the non-evasive procedure in the comfort of your home. A care plan is also provided post removal to ensure you are taking the right steps in the healing process.
